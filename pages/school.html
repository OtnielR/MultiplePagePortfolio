SMKN 6 TANGGERANG SELATAN

SMK Negeri 6 Kota Tangerang Selatan is a vocational high school that offers two fields of expertise: Computer and Informatics Technology, as well as Creative Arts and Industry. SMK Negeri 6 Kota Tangerang Selatan has three expertise programs/majors: Computer and Telecommunication Network Engineering, Software and Game Development, and Television Program Production and Broadcasting.


SMK Negeri 6 Kota Tangerang Selatan has been established since 2016. The school building is a three-story structure with a total area of 3,000 m², built on a 4,000 m² plot of land. SMK Negeri 6 Kota Tangerang Selatan was founded based on the Establishment Decree issued on June 9, 2015, with the number 800/1740.1-Dispend/201.
